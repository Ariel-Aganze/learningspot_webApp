The Challenge

Unemployment remains an alarming issue globally. In Rwanda, it is a particularly pressing concern, with a study conducted in Q1 2024 showing that "approximately one person was unemployed for every eight in the labor force."

Gender disparities persist, with females experiencing a higher unemployment rate at 14.5% compared to males at 11.5%. Youth face a notably higher rate of 16.6% compared to adults at 10.3%, and urban areas bear a heavier burden with an unemployment rate of 14% compared to 12.3% in rural areas.

Our Solution

This unemployment issue is linked to skill gaps, especially in digital skills and English fluency. This is where LearningSpot steps in, offering essential skills to help everyone compete successfully in the labor market.

Beyond tackling unemployment, Learning Spot is committed to supporting you all the way. We know the skills needed to succeed at work, and our training programs are designed to help you excel in your career.

For businesspeople and travelers, LearningSpot has you covered as well. You can learn new languages and master digital tools, connecting with loved ones and partners worldwide, all from the comfort of where you are.
